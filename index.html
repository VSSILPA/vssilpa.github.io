About

I am an ELLIS Ph.D. student specializing in trustworthy and responsible generative AI, co-advised by Dr. Anjan Dutta (University of Surrey), Prof. Serge Belongie (University of Copenhagen), and Dr. Muhammad Awais (University of Surrey). I am affiliated with the Surrey Institute for People-Centred AI, Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), and the Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence in Copenhagen. Before my Ph.D., I worked as a researcher (2019–2022) at Tata Research and Innovation Labs under Dr. Shirish Subhash Karande.

My research addresses trustworthiness and equity in generative AI, with a focus on diffusion model interpretability, fairness, and bias mitigation in text-to-image generation. My long-term vision is to build interpretability tools that give us a deeper understanding of what AI systems learn, and use that understanding as a lever to systematically improve fairness, reliability, and safety across a wide range of applications.
